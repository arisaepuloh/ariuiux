Hasanah Kebab – Website UMKM

Role: UI/UX Designer

This project is a user interface and user experience design for the Hasanah Kebab MSME website, a local kebab brand aiming to strengthen its identity in the digital space. The design goal is to present a modern, professional, and responsive look, while still maintaining a warm and appetizing feel that matches the product’s character.

During the design :

  • Research & Analysis: Understanding the target user profile and MSME business needs.
  • Wireframe & Layout: Creating a simple and easy-to-navigate page structure.
  • Visual Design: Using the brand’s signature colors (red & yellow) to build visual identity consistency.
  • Responsive: Ensuring an optimal experience across various devices (desktop & mobile).
  • Product Focus: Highlighting large product photos and clear call-to-actions
